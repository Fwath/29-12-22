Introducing the 2021 Audi e-tron RS: An electric car that packs a serious punch.

  • Make & Model:
    Audi eTron RS
  • Price:
    £145,000

Introducing the 2021 Audi e-tron RS: an electric car that packs a serious punch. This powerful performance electric SUV is sure to turn heads on the road with its sleek design and powerful acceleration. Combining the latest in electric vehicle technology with luxurious styling, the Audi e-tron RS is the perfect combination of power and sophistication. With a range of up to 302 miles and a top speed of 155 mph, the Audi e-tron RS offers an exhilarating driving experience.

In this review, we'll take a closer look at what makes the Audi e-tron RS so special.

The 2021 Audi e-tron RS is a stunning electric car that is sure to turn heads. With its sleek and modern design, this car definitely looks the part. The exterior features Audi's trademark Single frame grille, striking contours, and distinct matrix LED headlights. The exterior also has 21-inch alloy wheels, giving it an even more sporty look. The overall design of the car is one that is both elegant and aggressive. It's a perfect combination of luxury and performance. The Audi e-tron RS also comes with a range of exterior colours, including grey, black, blue, and silver. This provides customers with the flexibility to choose the colour that best suits their style. Overall, the exterior of the 2021 Audi e-tron RS is nothing short of spectacular. With its cutting-edge design, it's sure to be a showstopper wherever it goes.

The interior of the 2021 Audi e-tron RS is designed with the driver in mind. It features a 12.3-inch digital instrument cluster that is customizable and can be configured to display any information the driver desires. The RS also has a 10.1-inch touchscreen MMI infotainment system, which includes Android Auto and Apple CarPlay. Additionally, the RS has a dual-screen virtual cockpit, allowing the driver to switch between different displays on the same screen. The RS also comes with an optional four-zone climate control system, with separate controls for the driver, front passenger, and rear seats. There are also heated seats for the driver and front passenger, as well as ventilated seats for the rear passengers. Additionally, the interior is fitted with ambient lighting and aluminium accents, giving it a luxurious feel. Finally, there is plenty of storage space in the trunk and in the glove box.

The 2021 Audi e-tron RS is the top of the line for Audi's electric car range and it certainly does not disappoint. This car packs a serious punch when it comes to performance, offering up to 469 horsepower and 472 pound-feet of torque with overboost. This power output allows the e-tron RS to accelerate from 0 to 60 miles per hour in a blistering 3.5 seconds and reach a top speed of 155 mph. This car also offers an all-wheel drive system, which uses two electric motors to provide the necessary power and control. The Audi Quattro system provides increased grip and stability while cornering, allowing the e-tron RS to handle like a sports car. The car also features adaptive air suspension and active anti-roll bars, which further enhance its performance. The e-tron RS also features regenerative braking that helps to conserve energy and reduce brake wear. This is especially useful on longer drives, as it helps to improve overall efficiency. Overall, the Audi e-tron RS is an impressive performer with plenty of power and agility. It has been designed for those who want the performance of a sports car but with the added benefit of an electric drivetrain. If you're looking for an electric car that can pack a serious punch, then the Audi e-tron RS is definitely worth considering.

The 2021 Audi e-tron RS will be available at an MSRP of $109,000. This price point puts it in the upper echelons of electric vehicles, but it is worth every penny for the features and performance that you get with it. It offers three trims: the Premium Plus, Prestige, and First Edition. The Premium Plus trim comes with 20-inch wheels, LED headlights and taillights, a panoramic sunroof, and a 10-speaker Bang & Olufsen audio system. The Prestige trim adds 21-inch wheels, a head-up display, virtual side mirrors, heated and ventilated front seats, a heated steering wheel, and a four-zone climate control system. The First Edition comes with all of the features from the other trims plus exclusive exterior and interior colours, a 3D sound system, and an upgraded battery pack that provides an estimated range of 250 miles per charge. For those who want more features, there are numerous optional packages available. The Driver Assistance Package adds blind spot monitoring, adaptive cruise control, lane keeping assist, and rear cross traffic alert. The Comfort Package includes heated rear seats and a wireless smartphone charging pad. The Parking Assistance Package includes park distance control, rear collision avoidance assist, and a 360-degree parking camera. Overall, the 2021 Audi e-tron RS is an impressive vehicle that offers great performance and plenty of options for customization. With its luxurious interior and powerful engine, it is sure to be a favourite among car enthusiasts.
